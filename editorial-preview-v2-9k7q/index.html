Sync radio and TV in two simple steps

Tap Sync when you hear a recognizable moment on the radio. When you see that same moment on TV, tap Sync again. The app calculates the delay and adjusts playback so the radio matches the picture. After that, you can fine-tune the result with + and –.

Fine-tune with +, –, +10s and –10s

Use + and – to correct any small mismatch between sound and TV while the match continues. Use +10s and –10s when you need faster changes. If you want to start again, reset the delay to zero with D→0.

Create, manage and share your station list

Save your favorite stations and organize several URLs or servers for the same station. You can add URLs manually, import lists, export them and share them. The app is built for users who want to control which radio they listen to, not depend on a closed preset list.

An app born from a real need

Soccer RadioTv Sync was born from a very specific situation: watching matches on TV while listening to the radio commentator who had accompanied the user for years. As sports moved to streaming TV, the picture often started arriving later than online radio, so commentary could reveal goals and key moments before they appeared on screen.

Without finding a simple iPhone solution, a private prototype came first. Later, a private iPhone version was built, followed by the first commercial release. Version 2.0 is the result of that evolution: a clearer, more visual and more powerful app for bringing a classic way of enjoying sport into the streaming era.

Frequently asked questions

What is Soccer RadioTv Sync for?

It lets you listen to a compatible online radio stream synchronized with the TV picture while watching sport, especially when streaming TV arrives later than online radio.

Does the app include stations?

The app lets you search public stations through an integrated finder based on Radio Browser. You can also add your own compatible URLs manually.

Can I use any radio station?

You can use compatible online audio streams. Some stations, creators and services provide streams that can be added manually if they are compatible with the app playback system.

What if one URL is late?

You can try another URL or server for the same station. Different servers can have different latency.

Do I need an account?

No. You do not need to create an account.

Is there a subscription?

No. The app offers a 7-day free trial and then a one-time lifetime Premium unlock.

What happens if I already bought the app before?

Previous paid users receive lifetime Premium automatically.

Does it work best with streaming TV?

That is the main scenario. With streaming TV, the picture may arrive later than online radio, and the app can delay the radio to synchronize it with the picture.

What if I watch through terrestrial TV or satellite?

With terrestrial broadcast or satellite, the picture often arrives before online radio. In that case, Soccer RadioTv Sync may not be the ideal scenario, because it is designed mainly to delay the radio when the TV picture arrives later.

Soccer RadioTv Sync is designed for iPhone. Availability, trial and pricing may vary by country.
